【笔记】IDEA开启参数名提示

2023-03-17

前言

IDEA开启Java实参的参数名提示

临时查看参数名

  • Ctrl（Command）+P

永久显示参数名

  • Editor->Inlay Hints->Java->勾选Parameters with names that are contained in the method name和Complex expressions: binary, functional, array access and other->OK

  • 重启IDEA生效

完成
